## Meme Coins & Crypto Conflicts of Interest

### [2025-01-17] Trump Launches $TRUMP Meme Coin Days Before Inauguration
WHAT: Donald Trump launched a personal cryptocurrency meme coin ($TRUMP) on January 17, 2025, three days before his inauguration, with the token reaching a $72 billion market cap overnight; entities affiliated with the Trump Organization hold 80% of the coin's supply and collect transaction fees.
SOURCE: [NPR - What to know about Trump cryptocurrency meme coins](https://www.npr.org/2025/01/20/nx-s1-5268759/donald-trump-melania-cryptocurrency-meme-coins)

### [2025-01-19] Melania Trump Launches $MELANIA Meme Coin
WHAT: Melania Trump launched her own meme coin ($MELANIA) two days after her husband's, causing $TRUMP to drop 36%; ethics experts raised concerns about a sitting president and first lady personally profiting from speculative crypto assets while simultaneously setting crypto regulatory policy.
SOURCE: [Axios - Melania Trump launches MELANIA meme coin](https://www.axios.com/2025/01/19/melania-trump-meme-coin-crypto)

### [2025-01-23] Trump Signs Executive Order Creating Crypto Working Group
WHAT: Trump signed an executive order establishing a Presidential Working Group on Digital Asset Markets, overturning Biden-era crypto regulations and forbidding work on a central bank digital currency, while his family maintained extensive personal crypto holdings.
SOURCE: [Axios - Trump creates crypto group](https://www.axios.com/2025/01/23/trump-bitcoin-strategic-reserve-order)

### [2025-02-28] SEC Drops Civil Fraud Case Against Justin Sun, Major Trump Crypto Investor
WHAT: The SEC, now under Trump appointees, agreed to pause and ultimately drop its civil fraud case against crypto mogul Justin Sun, who had invested $75 million into the Trump family-backed World Liberty Financial token, with up to $56 million of that going to the Trump family as fees.
SOURCE: [CNN - A crypto mogul who invested millions into Trump coins is getting a reprieve on civil fraud charges](https://www.cnn.com/2025/02/28/business/crypto-mogul-trump-coins-civil-fraud-charges)

### [2025-03-06] Trump Establishes Strategic Bitcoin Reserve by Executive Order
WHAT: Trump signed an executive order creating a Strategic Bitcoin Reserve using approximately 200,000 government-held bitcoin from seizures, and a separate stockpile including Ethereum, Solana, XRP, and Cardano, while his family held billions in personal crypto assets.
SOURCE: [CNN - Trump creates a Strategic Bitcoin Reserve](https://www.cnn.com/2025/03/06/business/strategic-bitcoin-reserve-trump)

### [2025-04-07] Deputy AG Todd Blanche Halts Crypto Enforcement While Holding Crypto Assets
WHAT: Deputy Attorney General Todd Blanche issued a memo titled "Ending Regulation by Prosecution" halting Biden-era crypto investigations before divesting his $159,000-$485,000 in crypto holdings, violating his own ethics agreement to divest before participating in crypto-related matters.
SOURCE: [ProPublica - Top DOJ Official Shut Down Enforcement Against Crypto Companies While Holding More Than $150,000 in Crypto Investments](https://www.propublica.org/article/todd-blanche-crypto-doj-trump)

### [2025-04-08] DOJ Disbands Cryptocurrency Crimes Team
WHAT: The Justice Department disbanded its National Cryptocurrency Enforcement Team and ceased cryptocurrency enforcement actions, while the Trump family's crypto ventures were simultaneously expanding.
SOURCE: [PBS - Justice Department will disband its team focused on cryptocurrency crimes](https://www.pbs.org/newshour/politics/justice-department-will-disband-its-team-focused-on-cryptocurrency-crimes)

### [2025-05-02] Trump Family Reports $500 Million in Crypto Profits in First 100 Days
WHAT: Within the first 100 days of the administration, the Trump family had made approximately $500 million from World Liberty Financial, with the president's crypto holdings representing nearly 40% of his net worth (approximately $2.9 billion).
SOURCE: [Axios - Inside the Trump family's 100 days of presidential profit](https://www.axios.com/2025/05/02/trump-crypto-world-liberty-business-profit)

### [2025-05-06] Democrats Target Trump's Meme Coin and Crypto Empire in Legislation

WHAT: Trump hosted a black-tie dinner at his Virginia golf club for the top 220 holders of his $TRUMP meme coin, who collectively spent over $140 million for access; the top holder was Chinese-born crypto mogul Justin Sun (whose SEC fraud case was recently dropped), raising concerns about foreign nationals buying access to the president.

## Self-Dealing & Domestic Corruption

### [2025-01-24] Trump Fires 17 Inspectors General in Overnight Purge
WHAT: The Trump administration fired approximately 17 independent inspectors general at federal agencies effective immediately in a late Friday night purge, without providing Congress the legally required 30-day notice; even Republican Sen. WHAT: The Trump DOJ ordered prosecutors to drop federal corruption charges against NYC Mayor Eric Adams, citing interference with Trump's immigration agenda; seven prosecutors resigned rather than comply; a federal judge later dismissed the case "with prejudice," writing that the dismissal "smacks of a bargain" for immigration policy concessions.

WHAT: The Trump administration slashed the Justice Department's Public Integrity Section from approximately 35 lawyers to four or five, effectively gutting the unit that oversees prosecution of public officials accused of corruption; remaining cases were reassigned to local U.S. attorneys' offices.
SOURCE: [CNN - How Trump's Justice Department has gutted the government's ability to chase public corruption](https://www.cnn.com/2025/02/14/politics/corruption-justice-department-trump-new-york)

### [2025-04-09] Trump Posts "Great Time to Buy" Hours Before Announcing Tariff Pause

SOURCE: [NPR - How Trump family business ventures stand to directly benefit the President](https://www.npr.org/2025/05/07/nx-s1-5388474/how-trump-family-business-ventures-stand-to-directly-benefit-the-president)

## Tariff Chaos & Economic Impact

### [2025-04-02] "Liberation Day" Tariffs Announced on Dozens of Nations
WHAT: Trump announced sweeping tariffs including a 10% universal import duty and "reciprocal" tariffs on 90 nations (34% on China, 20% on EU, 32% on Taiwan, 25% on South Korea), approaching levels not seen since the Smoot-Hawley Tariff Act of 1930 that deepened the Great Depression.
SOURCE: [NPR - Trump announces reciprocal tariffs on dozens of nations](https://www.npr.org/2025/04/02/nx-s1-5345802/trump-tariffs-liberation-day)

### [2025-04-09] Trump Announces 90-Day Tariff Pause After Market Turmoil
WHAT: After days of severe market losses following "Liberation Day," Trump paused most new tariffs for 90 days while increasing China tariffs to 125%; the Dow surged nearly 3,000 points on the reversal, hours after Trump's "great time to buy" social media post.
SOURCE: [CBS News - Trump pauses most of his new tariffs for 90 days](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/trump-announces-90-day-tariffs-pause/)

### [2025-06-03] OECD Downgrades Economic Forecasts Due to Tariffs
WHAT: The OECD forecast U.S. GDP growth would slide to 1.6% in 2025 (from 2.8% in 2024), with inflation spiking to 3.9% by year-end; the trade deficit for the first half of 2025 was $582.7 billion, 38% higher than 2024, the opposite of Trump's stated goal.
SOURCE: [CNN - Trump's tariffs will hit the US and world economies harder than previously thought, OECD says](https://www.cnn.com/2025/06/03/economy/oecd-economic-downgrade-trump-tariffs-intl)

### [2025-08-07] Broad Tariffs Take Effect as Economic Pain Surfaces
WHAT: Tariffs on goods from more than 60 countries went into effect at rates of 10% or higher, with factory jobs declining rather than growing as promised, and companies hesitant to invest due to policy uncertainty.
SOURCE: [NPR - Trump's broad tariffs go into effect, just as economic pain is surfacing](https://www.npr.org/2025/08/07/nx-s1-5495218/trump-tariffs-trade-economy)
